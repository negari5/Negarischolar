# Negari Scholar Platform

**Dream Bold. Apply Smart.**

Negari Scholar is a student-first digital platform designed to guide African students, starting with Ethiopia, through the complex journey of discovering, preparing for, and winning scholarships. It acts as a smart companion from the moment a student dreams of studying abroad to the moment they submit applications and beyond.

Negari is not just a directory. It is a guided journey.

### 1. Smart Onboarding

Students begin by selecting:

* Country of interest
* Field of study
* Education level

### 2. Scholarship Discovery & Matching

* Curated and verified scholarship opportunities
* Filtered by eligibility, deadlines, and goals
* Focused on real, achievable opportunities

### 3. Readiness & Preparation Tools

* Application readiness checklist
* Timeline tracking
* Required document guidance (CV, essays, recommendations)

* Tips on essays, interviews, and motivation letters
* Practical advice from real scholarship experiences

### 5. Application Tracking

* Track applied, upcoming, and missed scholarships
* Deadline reminders
* Progress visibility

### 6. Support System

* Family and peer encouragement features
* Community-driven motivation
* Human-centered guidance through the Negari Assistant

### 7. Post-Application Follow-Up

* Next steps after submission
* Interview preparation
* Decision tracking
